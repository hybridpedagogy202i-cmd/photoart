## 💡 Usage Tips

1. **Aspect Ratios**: Specify based on use case
   - Hero images: 16:9 or 21:9
   - Project thumbnails: 4:3 or 1:1
   - Portraits: 3:4 or 9:16

2. **Style Keywords to Mix**:
   - "Award-winning", "Dribbble trending", "Behance featured"
   - "Minimalist", "Brutalist", "Neumorphic", "Glassmorphic"
   - "Cinematic", "Editorial", "Gallery quality"

3. **Technical Specs**:
   - Always request "ultra high definition" or "8K"
   - Mention "professional portfolio quality"
   - Specify color codes for brand consistency

4. **Iterative Refinement**:
   - Start broad, then refine based on results
   - Combine elements from multiple prompts
   - Adjust lighting, mood, and composition keywords
